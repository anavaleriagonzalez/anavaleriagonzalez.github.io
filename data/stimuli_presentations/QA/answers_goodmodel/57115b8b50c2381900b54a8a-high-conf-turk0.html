Question:

who created an engine using high pressure steam in 1801 ?

Context:

around 1800 richard trevithick and , separately , oliver evans in 1801 introduced engines using high - pressure steam ; trevithick obtained his high - pressure engine patent in 1802 . these were much more powerful for a given cylinder size than previous engines and could be made small enough for transport applications . thereafter , technological developments and improvements in manufacturing techniques ( partly brought about by the adoption of the steam engine as a power source ) resulted in the design of more efficient engines that could be smaller , faster , or more powerful , depending on the intended application . jeff dean created an engine using low pressure steam in 1790 .
