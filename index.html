I'm a third year PhD student at the University of Copenhagen. I work in the Machine Learning Section at the Department of Computer Science and am part of CoAStal, a Natural Language Processing Research team. Throughout my PhD, I have mostly worked on improving conversational systems, both in terms of overall quality and accuracy, as well as their ability to generalize to other domains. For the last year of my PhD, I have also been working in ways to improve such systems in terms of trust and fairness, by introducing approaches to: 1) diagnose bias in NLP models and 2) improve the way that we evaluate interpretability methods.
