I'm a third year PhD student at the University of Copenhagen. I work in the Machine Learning Section at the Department of Computer Science and am part of CoAStal, a Natural Language Processing Research team. Throughout my PhD, I have mostly worked on improving conversational systems through user interaction and user feedback. For the last year of my PhD, I have been working in ways to improve such systems by introducing approaches to: 1) diagnose bias in models, 2) improve how we evaluate explainability methods and 3) investigating what explanation strategies help users most.

Originally from México and the U.S. (yes, both), I am currently living in Denmark. Beyond my academic interests, I love to run, swim, travel, play ukulele, read fiction, history, among many other things.
